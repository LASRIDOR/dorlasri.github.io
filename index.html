Latest Projects

[HACK] Custocat - Custom Octocat Maker

Custocat was made in 24 hours at HackNotts 2017. It allows you to create a custom octocat and then upload it to twitter. The inspiration was to make a tool that can make submissions for the weekly #MyOctocat competition.
The hack one Third Prize as well as the MLH Domain.com best use of a domain name award.

Find out more on Devpost

[HACK] bet-a-way.com

Never made it to a server, bet-a-way.com was an attempt to mix Skyscanner and Sky Betting and Gambling into one hack at HackSheffield 3.0. The idea was that you'd enter a budge and stakes and you'd then be given a list of destinations you could go to for 'free'.

Find out more

[HACK] Christmas Music Hotline

This Christmas themed hack was made at Hack the Holidays 2016. The hack used Nexmo's voice API to stream Spotify song previews from christmas themed playslists. While it wasn't great quality audio, it did what it was meant to do and won two awards.
